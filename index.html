My research focuses on Biomedical Image Analysis with healthcare applications. With experience in computer vision, machine learning, computer graphics and visualization, I work on the end-to-end computation pipeline: data processing, reconstruction and analysis.

My previous works include Arrow of Time, MitoEM Benchmark, Biology-aware Skeletonization.
