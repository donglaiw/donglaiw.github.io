I spent memorable postdoc years with Hanspeter Pfister at Harvard University. I was fortunate to be supervised by William Freeman during my Ph.D. study at MIT CSAIL. I finished my undergraduate in Maths from Brown University (transferred from Tsinghua University), where I was privileged to work under Charles Lawrence, Erik Sudderth, Stuart Geman, and See-Chen Ying.


Research

Our group studies the interplay between biological sciences and computer vision: building computational models for biomedical image analysis and examining brain mechanisms to guide computer video understanding.
  •   - Biomedical: Connectomics [Google AI blog], CyCIF
  •   - Video understanding: Arrow of Time, Vimeo-90K, Motion Microscopy
