My group advances AI4Neuroscience by developing foundation models for large-scale biomedical image analysis and investigating causal mechanisms in complex systems. Our research is generously supported by NSF CAREER Award, NIH R01, and Gates Foundation.

I spent memorable postdoc years at Harvard University, working with Hanspeter Pfister and Jeff Lichtman. I was fortunate to be advised by William Freeman during my Ph.D. at MIT CSAIL. As an undergraduate in Mathematics at Brown University (after transferring from Tsinghua University), I had the privilege to work under Charles Lawrence, Erik Sudderth, Stuart Geman, and See-Chen Ying.

Now Looking For: postdocs, visiting scholars and research interns.
