My research interest lies in the Biomedical Image Analysis with healthcare applications. Trained in computer vision, machine learning, computer graphics and visualization, I work on data processing, reconstruction and analysis.

During my Ph.D, I enjoyed the fun projects to examine the arrow of time from videos.
